One Piece - A Timeless Classic

One Piece is manga created by Eiichiro Oda in 1997. The story follows a boy named Monkey D. Luffy whose dream is to become the Pirate King. In order to do this, he must get the legendary treasure, the One Piece. In the overall narrative, he must overcome the challenges/enemies he faces along the Grand Line to succeed his dream.


Why I like it

  • It's a broad and interesting story.
  • It follows dark themes but with a light tone.
  • It has a very big community.
